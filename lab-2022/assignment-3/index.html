Collective Self

Conceptual Origins

I began this project by brainstorming and reflecting
on what digital worlds I have belonged to throughout
my life. I found that much of the worlds I enjoyed
spending time in throughout my childhood had to do
with personalizing avatars, competing with friends,
and playing any kind of internet game

One kind of the place I would always spend hours on when
I was younger were those websites that had an array of
games like chess, sudoko, crosswords, and word searches.
Using that as my base, I began to brainstorm a way to
novelize those games and include an avatar.
