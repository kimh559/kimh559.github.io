Hope's Recipe

This webpage is part of project 2: Dinner for Two. It is a creative recipe that focuses on pulling together typography, stylesheet, and what defines a "recipe".

  • three cups of rhythym
  • one teaspoon of reality
  • 1/2 cup of melancholy
  • 2 1/2 cups of emotional intelligence
  • 1 tablespoon of contentness
  • 2 dashes of dramatic endings

Instructions

  1. honey cakes so soft to the touch glassy fruit laid out a-front teacups delicate and sweet smelling herbs along with my tea steam does the air around me stir
  2. white lace dances along the table cloth and whistling winds chatter with trees the oven's excitement brings about aromas of love as my window welcomes home the sun rays from above
  3. my guests range in sizes and smiles eager to share beautiful flowers of wild we sit and serve surrounded with petals and away our sadnesses fly, to our dreams in which we revel
  4. tart flavors fade into memories of haze we creatures love our afternooning through the day a celebration of food and flight and of course dancing to prove off our superiority of the jive
  5. once again the sunset graces our eyes her gentle dipping into the horizon which we so admire our sadnesses which had lingered for so long follow her home to come back another day
